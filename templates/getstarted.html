What Does PulmoGuard Do?

PulmoGuard is an AI-powered pneumonia detection tool designed to assist doctors, patients, and healthcare providers in identifying early signs of pneumonia. By combining chest X-ray analysis with a smart symptom checker, PulmoGuard makes pneumonia detection faster, more accessible, and reliable. Our mission is to support medical professionals and empower patients with quick, accurate insights.
